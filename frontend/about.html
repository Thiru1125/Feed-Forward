About FeedForward

A technology-driven initiative to reduce food waste and fight hunger.

FeedForward is a food redistribution platform developed as a mini project to minimize food wastage and help feed people in need. The system connects food donors such as restaurants, events, and individuals with NGOs and shelters efficiently.

Our Objectives

Reduce Food Waste

Ensure surplus food is not discarded but redistributed responsibly.

Support NGOs

Help NGOs and shelters get timely access to excess food.

Serve the Needy

Ensure food reaches people who need it the most.
